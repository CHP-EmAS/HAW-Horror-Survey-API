Architectural Elements in Gaming: A Quantitative Analysis of Atmospheric Impact

Welcome to our survey! This study aims to investigate whether the architectural design within a game alone can evoke a sense of unease or tension. In this survey, you will be evaluating 15 levels from both horror and non-horror games. Your feedback is crucial in understanding the impact of architectural elements on the overall atmosphere.

Survey Duration:

The survey is estimated to take approximately 10-15 minutes of your time.

Data Privacy:

Rest assured, your responses will be anonymized, and no personally identifiable information will be stored. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

Optional Email for Results:

At the end of the survey, you have the option to provide your email address if you wish to receive the survey results. Please note that this information will be kept separate from your survey responses for confidentiality.
